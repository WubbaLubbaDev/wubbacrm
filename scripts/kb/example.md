# About WubbaCRM

WubbaCRM is a customer relationship management tool for small sales teams.
Replace this file with your real knowledge-base content — company info, FAQs,
service descriptions, pricing, and policies. Each `.md` file in this folder
becomes one document; the seed script chunks and embeds them for RAG.

## Hours & Contact

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Asia/Jakarta).
You can reach us at support@example.com.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I book a demo?**
Ask the assistant to schedule a demo and it will check available calendar slots.

**What languages are supported?**
The assistant can chat in both Bahasa Indonesia and English.
