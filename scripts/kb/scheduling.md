# Scheduling a Meeting

Visitors can book a 30-minute meeting with any of our team members through this
chat. When you ask about availability, the assistant checks that team member's
live calendar and suggests open time slots. To confirm a booking, provide your
name and email.

The assistant knows which team members are available and will list them if you
are not sure who to meet with — for example, you can ask for a specific person
by name, or by what you need help with, and the assistant will suggest who to
talk to.

## Availability

- **Working hours:** 09:00–17:00 WIB (Asia/Jakarta time).
- **Meeting length:** 30 minutes per appointment.
- **How far ahead:** You can book within the next few days. For dates further
  out, please email us and we'll arrange it manually.
- Each team member's real calendar is checked at the moment you ask, so only
  genuinely free slots are offered.

## How to book

1. Tell the assistant you'd like to schedule a meeting, and who with (e.g.
   "I'd like to book a call with [name]" or "Who can I talk to about pricing?").
2. If you didn't name a person, the assistant lists the available team members
   so you can choose.
3. The assistant shows that person's open 30-minute slots.
4. Pick a slot and give your **name** and **email**.
5. The assistant confirms the appointment and adds it to that team member's
   calendar.

## Example questions

- "Who can I schedule a meeting with?"
- "Is [name] free on Sunday?"
- "Can I book a 30-minute call this week?"
- "Show me available slots tomorrow morning."

## Contact

If no suitable slot is available, email support@example.com and we'll find a time
that works.

# Menjadwalkan Pertemuan (Bahasa Indonesia)

Pengunjung dapat memesan pertemuan 30 menit dengan salah satu anggota tim kami
melalui chat ini. Saat Anda menanyakan ketersediaan, asisten akan memeriksa
kalender anggota tim tersebut secara langsung dan menyarankan slot waktu yang
kosong. Untuk konfirmasi, berikan nama dan email Anda.

Jika Anda belum yakin ingin bertemu dengan siapa, asisten akan menampilkan
daftar anggota tim yang tersedia.

## Ketersediaan

- **Jam kerja:** 09:00–17:00 WIB (Asia/Jakarta).
- **Durasi pertemuan:** 30 menit per janji temu.
- **Rentang waktu:** Anda dapat memesan untuk beberapa hari ke depan.
- Kalender setiap anggota tim diperiksa secara real-time, jadi hanya slot yang
  benar-benar kosong yang ditawarkan.

## Cara memesan

1. Beri tahu asisten bahwa Anda ingin menjadwalkan pertemuan, dan dengan siapa
   (mis. "Saya ingin booking call dengan [nama]").
2. Jika Anda tidak menyebut nama, asisten akan menampilkan anggota tim yang
   tersedia untuk Anda pilih.
3. Asisten menampilkan slot 30 menit yang tersedia milik orang tersebut.
4. Pilih slot dan berikan **nama** dan **email** Anda.
5. Asisten mengonfirmasi janji temu dan menambahkannya ke kalender anggota tim.

## Contoh pertanyaan

- "Saya bisa menjadwalkan pertemuan dengan siapa?"
- "Apakah [nama] kosong hari Minggu?"
- "Bisa booking call 30 menit minggu ini?"
